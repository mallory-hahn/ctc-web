quantitative trading analyst


As a QTA, you are responsible for managing risk, interacting with brokers, pricing options, and continuously growing and learning.


Never heard of an option before? No problem! Rarely do QTAs have a financial background. We are looking for highly quantitative individuals with a desire to learn.


A typical day for a QTA can involve everything from working on long term projects to making trades or understanding why certain trades were made

software engineer


As a Software Engineer at CTC, you are writing the code that keeps the company running.


You could be working on everything from the databases that store the abundance of data that is collected each day to the algorithms and software that make trading possible.


All users of your software are internal; you get to see your code live on past the final push to production and watch it actually have an immediate impact on the company.
